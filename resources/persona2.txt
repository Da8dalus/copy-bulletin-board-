Dr. Emmie Cook is a principal at an elementary school. Dr. Cook has to release information consistently and effectively to the parents of the students in her school. This involves important safety imformation, schedule reminders, and school-wide event notices. She often recieves complaints that automated calls from the school disrupt the parents work day and direct them to a confusing website where information is hard to find. Dr. Cook wants a way to effeciently and effectively release information, so that the school's office is not bogged down by unecessary calls.
